Pasta

Description:

The particular pasta that we will be perparing in this case has an alfredo sauce, uses medium shells, and has comprises chicken, mushrooms and red onions as core ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Red onions, sliced.
  • Fresh basil.
  • Alfredo cheese sauce.
  • Grilled chicken.
  • Baby bella mushrooms, whole.
  • Medium pasta shells.

Steps to prepare:

  1. Cube the chicken breasts.
  2. Slice the red onion.
  3. Cook the onion and chicken in a large pan.
  4. Boil the pasta shells.
  5. Add the alfredo sauce and the mushrooms to the large pan.
  6. Spice to taste and serve.
