Ceviche

Description:

Ceviche is a dish consisting of onions, seafood, and cilantro typically - all "cooked" with citric acid from lime juice. Best served cold with tortilla chips.

Ingredients:

  • Red and white onions, diced.
  • Fresh cilantro.
  • Lemon and lime juice.
  • Peeled shrimp.
  • Fresh talapia and/or cod.
  • Thick, crispy tortilla chips.

Steps to prepare:

  1. Peel the shrimp.
  2. Cube the shrimp and fish into medium sized pieces.
  3. Submerge the seafood into the citric acid.
  4. Prep the onions while the seafood is cooked by the citric acid.
  5. Add larges sums of cilantro to the dish.
  6. Refridgerate for 30 minutes before serving.
