Curry

Description:

Curry is a traditional indian dish that contains a wide array of spices and can easily be adapted to ominvore, vegetarian, and vegan crowds.

Ingredients:

  • Yellow Onion.
  • Garam Masala.
  • Coconut Milk.
  • Carrots.
  • Potato.
  • Mushrooms.
  • Basmati Rice or Naan Bread.

Steps to prepare:

  1. Toast the spices in a pan, stirring quickly.
  2. Add olive oil and begin heating the onions, potato, and carrot.
  3. Begin to boil the rice.
  4. Add the mushrooms to the pan.
  5. Add the coconut milk to the pan.
  6. Simmer 30 minutes and serve with the rice.
